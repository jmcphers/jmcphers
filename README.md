Hi, I'm Jonathan.  I'm a software architect at [Posit PBC](https://posit.co), currently working on [Positron](https://positron.posit.co), a polyglot data science IDE. My past projects include [the RStudio IDE](https://posit.co/products/open-source/rstudio/?sid=1), [Posit Workbench](https://posit.co/products/enterprise/workbench/), and [Microsoft Access](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/access). 

I've always done full-stack engineering, and these days am writing mostly Rust for Positron's backend components, including its [R kernel](https://github.com/posit-dev/ark) and [Jupyter kernel supevisor](https://github.com/posit-dev/kallichore), and Node/Typescript/React for its front end.

I love building and thinking about tools for people, and have spent most of my career on desktop data and productivity software; in the age of agentic coding and automated implementation, the importance of taste and good design choices has risen dramatically. I went on about this at some length in a recent [keynote speech at posit conf](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tGrePGYj7Uw).
